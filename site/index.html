Why Adaptive π

No Tessellation

Analytic curves and surfaces mean smooth geometry at any zoom level without triangle meshes.

Infinite Scaling

Resolution-independent rendering and export for fabrication, printing, or AR/VR twins.

Multi-Dimensional

Grade-aware algebra unlocks operations beyond 3D—time, fields, and constraints live together.

Projected Wedge Algebra (⋆)

Grade-2 closure with antisymmetric interactions and a compact Λ²V representation.

Civil Add-On

Alignment accuracy for construction survey modeling, corridor design, and field validation.

Playground App

Parametric editors, curve libraries, STL repair, and export tuned for creators.

Pure math. Real hardware.

Adaptive π fuses analytic geometry with tangible fabrication: analytic rendering on CPUs today, GPUs tomorrow; ER-fluid control, CNC, and robotics aligned to the same math library.

Roadmap Highlights

  • Alpha Playground: Parametric editor, inspector, and analytic rendering with export.
  • Civil Toolkit: Corridor alignment, station equations, offset surfaces, and data QA.
  • Open Plugins: Blender / FreeCAD connectors, AMA SDK, and Python bindings.
  • Field Ops: GNSS + lidar ingestion, job replay, and AR overlays.

Tech Stack

  • Core Library: Adaptive π projected wedge algebra with analytic kernels.
  • Rendering: CPU analytic renderer now; GPU acceleration and game engine integration next.
  • Interoperability: AMA, ACProj, IFC, LandXML, and STL repair pipelines.
  • Collaboration: Backer portal with nightly builds, tutorials, and surveys.
